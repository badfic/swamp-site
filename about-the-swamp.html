All About Us

Hello all you cool cats and kittens and welcome to The Swamp!

What originally started out as a server named Rarepair Affair for this discussion of rarepairs in fanfiction, has grown into something a whole lot larger than itself.

The Swamp went through a lot of growing pains in its heyday. Back when we were barely more than a few months old and just a bunch of chaotic queers all cooped up during a pandemic, just wanting to connect with like-minded people so we could feel less alone. What many of us found was a family, a place for all of us to come together and talk about the things we love and the things that ail us.

Because who is more supportive of your neroses than a bunch of nerds from all over the world experiencing the same things?

Now, The Swamp is a space for us to come together to watch TV shows, movies, play games, talk trivia, run writing sprints, track our writing progress, and more. We've become a place that's for everyone. No matter where you come from or who you are.

If you love something and you want to scream about it or if you just want a group of nerds who will love and support youi through these unprecedented times, The Swamp is the place for you.
